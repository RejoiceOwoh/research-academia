Meet Our Editor-in-Chief

Prof. Titus Maduabuchi Owoh

Dean, Faculty of Education, Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT)  •  Editor-in-Chief, Research Academia Journals

Prof. T.M. Owoh leads Research Academia's editorial vision. As Dean of the Faculty of Education, ESUT (August 2024 – August 2026), he champions the International Journal of Integrated Research in Education (IJIRE) — a Faculty-wide, peer-reviewed journal. Earlier, as Head of the Department of Technical and Vocational Education (TVE), ESUT, he founded and hosted the departmental journals INTERJ and JORSTED, building a research culture that now spans the entire Faculty.
