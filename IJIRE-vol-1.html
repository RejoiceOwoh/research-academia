International Journal of Integrated Research in Education (IJIRE)

An international, peer-reviewed journal published by the Faculty of Education, Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT), Enugu, Nigeria. This inaugural issue brings together 46 research articles on Artificial Intelligence in Nigerian secondary schools and higher education, experiential learning, virtual laboratory instruction, educational leadership and hyper-local classroom research across the Enugu, Agbani, Awgu and Kwale Education Zones.

  Editorial Board

Editor-in-Chief: Professor Titus Maduabuchi Owoh (Prof. T.M. Owoh) — Dean, Faculty of Education, Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT), Enugu, Nigeria (August 2024 – August 2026).

Editorial Desk: Faculty of Education, Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT), Enugu, Nigeria. IJIRE is a Faculty-wide peer-reviewed journal.
