Association of Business Educators of Nigeria, Enugu State chapter was inaugrated on 12th May, 2022. It is a professional Association committed to the promotion and advancement of Business Education in Enugu State, Nigeria. The major objectives of the Association are to: Bring Business educators in Nigeria together, promote professional growth of thier members, arrange for conferences, symposia, workshops, seminars and publications.
